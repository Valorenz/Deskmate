OVERVIEW
This document provides troubleshooting steps for remote employees experiencing connectivity issues with the Epson Corporate VPN (GlobalProtect/Pulse Secure).

TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS:
1. Verify Your Internet Connection:
   - Ensure your local Wi-Fi or ethernet connection is stable.
   - Try browsing to a public website (e.g., google.com) to confirm access.
2. Check VPN Credentials:
   - Ensure your Active Directory credentials are correct.
   - If your password was recently changed, update it in your VPN client settings.
3. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):
   - Make sure you approve the MFA push notification on your mobile device within 30 seconds.
4. Clear DNS Cache:
   - Open Command Prompt (Windows) and type: ipconfig /flushdns
   - Open Terminal (macOS) and type: sudo dscacheutil -flushcache; sudo killall -HUP mDNSResponder
5. Change Gateway:
   - If the primary gateway is slow, open the VPN client and switch to the secondary gateway (e.g., vpn-sec.epson.com).
6. Restart Services:
   - Restart the VPN client application.
   - If issues persist, reboot your computer.

For assistance, contact NetOps at netops@epson.co.id or open a high-priority ticket in DeskMate.
