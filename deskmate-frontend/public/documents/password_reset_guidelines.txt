OVERVIEW
This document outlines the standard operating procedure for resetting employee credentials across Epson local and network domains.

STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE:
1. Access the self-service reset portal at https://sso.epson-secure.com
2. Enter your employee email address (e.g. employee@epson.co.id).
3. Complete the multi-factor authentication (MFA) prompt sent to your registered mobile phone or authenticator app.
4. Input a new password that meets the security policy requirements:
   - Minimum of 12 characters.
   - At least 1 uppercase and 1 lowercase letter.
   - At least 1 numerical digit.
   - At least 1 special character (e.g., !, @, #, $, %, etc.).
   - Must not contain your username, first name, or last name.
   - Must not match any of your previous 5 passwords.
5. If you are locked out of your account, contact the IT Service Desk directly via extension 8888 or create a Helpdesk ticket in DeskMate.

For additional safety guidelines, please refer to the IT Security Handbook.
